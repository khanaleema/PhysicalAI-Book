# Chapter 03: Service and Personal Applications

## Overview

This chapter examines how humanoid robots are being used in service industries and personal applications, from hospitality and retail to home assistance and personal care. It explores the unique requirements and challenges of human-robot interaction in these contexts.

## Learning Objectives

* Understand service robot applications
* Learn hospitality use cases
* Explore retail applications
* Master personal assistant robots
* Understand human interaction requirements

## Core Concepts

**Hotel Services:**
- Guest assistance
- Room service delivery
- Concierge services
- Information provision
- Entertainment

**Restaurant Services:**
- Taking orders

- Bussing tables

- Menu recommendations
