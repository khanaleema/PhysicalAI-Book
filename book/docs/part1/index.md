Physical AI represents a paradigm shift from software-only intelligence to systems that interact with the physical world. Unlike traditional AI that processes data, Physical AI systems:

1. **Perceive** their environment through sensors
2. **Reason** about actions using AI algorithms
3. **Act** upon the world through actuators and mechanisms
